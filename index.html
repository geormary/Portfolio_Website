I Also Go by Geo

Hi, I'm Geo—a software engineer and creative problem solver with a passion for blending technology and art. With over 3 years of experience working in the tech industry, I've contributed to innovative projects, including my work at Uber, where I developed and enhanced features that improved user experience and efficiency for drivers. Beyond my technical background, I’m also a philosopher at heart. I approach every challenge with a critical eye and a creative mindset, always striving to find solutions that push boundaries and spark growth. I believe in the power of communication and empathy, which has helped me build products that truly connect with users. When I’m not coding, you can find me diving into creative projects—whether it’s designing, sewing, or creating content. I’m deeply committed to personal development, business, and faith, and I’m on a mission to help others grow along their journeys as well. Through my upcoming Substack, I’ll be sharing insights on tech news, self-development, and business strategies.Let's connect and build something amazing together!
